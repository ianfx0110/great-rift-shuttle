Established 2010

Great Rift Shuttle was founded with a single goal: to provide the most reliable transport service between Nairobi and the Rift Valley region. Today, we have grown into one of Kenya's most trusted Saccos.

Our fleet of modern 14-seater shuttles and larger buses are equipped with the latest safety features and comfort amenities, ensuring that every journey you take with us is pleasant. We prioritize precision, punctuality, and traveler security above all else.
